O programa é constituído por convidados de diversas áreas (Design Gráfico, Design de Interacção, Web Design, Arquitectura, Teoria do Design, Arte, Ilustração, etc.) repartidos por quatro sessões. Ciclo de Conversas Design + Multimedia (CCDM) consists of a series of talks that take place annually during the months of April and May, organised within the scope of the Bachelor and Master courses in Design and Multimedia and the PhD in Computational Media Design at the University of Coimbra.
Organizing comittee João Miguel Cunha Pedro Martins Sérgio M. Rebelo Olga Marinho Barbara Nascimento Carolina Libório Cardoso
Visual Identity Ana Catarina Almeida Ana Leonor Brandão Rafael Teodoro Belas da Silva Maria Miguel Pedro
2022 Edition The 2021 edition of the Design + Multimedia Conversations series featured Katja Schloz, Tobias Wüstefeld, Elaine Ramos, Miguel Gouveia, Sofian Audry, Kaffe Matthews, Ece Canlı, and Diogo Tudela, exploring graphic and editorial design, illustration, sound, interaction, technology, and critical design research.
Organizing Committee Ana Boavida Fábio Gouveia João Couceiro e Castro João Miguel Cunha Laura Pais Mariana Seiça Nuno Coelho Pedro Martins Sérgio Rebelo Tiago Martins
2022 Edition In 2022, participants included Patrick Thomas, Nina Paim, André Rangel, Joana Monteiro, Adriana Sá, and Thomas Castro, addressing graphic design, research, creative processes, and experimental practices at the intersection of art and multimedia.
Organizing Committee Artur Rebelo Beatriz Ferreira João Miguel Cunha Maria Eduarda Kuzer Nathalie Jennings Nuno Coelho Pedro Martins Pedro Silva Sérgio M. Rebelo
2023 Edition The 2023 edition featured Current, André Rangel, Joost Grootens, and Giorgia Lupi, presenting projects and approaches in graphic design, visual thinking, and multidisciplinary practices, with a focus on the intersection of art, design, and technology.
Organizing Committee Gonçalo Figueiredo João Cunha Leonor Domingues Margarida Figueiredo Nuno Coelho Pedro Martins Sérgio Rebelo
