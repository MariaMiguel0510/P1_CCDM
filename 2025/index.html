Lena Weber Lena Weber is a graphic designer working between design and code. She focuses on the research and the development of design tools and typographic systems. She is most interested in the intersection of art, typography, technology, and philosophic theory and how they inspire visual communication.

Lisa Moura Lisa H. Moura is a Lisbon-based designer and founder of Frau im Mond, a graphic and digital design studio inspired by the visionary spirit of Frau im Mond by Fritz Lang. Her career includes collaborations with leading cultural institutions such as Fundação Calouste Gulbenkian, MAAT, and Museu Nacional de Arqueologia. In her talk, she shares highlights from her professional path and selected projects, reflecting on process, experimentation, and the development of a distinctive design practice.

Ciclo de Conversas Design + Multimedia (CCDM) consists of a series of talks that take place annually during the months of March and April, organised within the scope of the Bachelor and Master courses in Design and Multimedia and the PhD in Computational Media Design at the University of Coimbra.
